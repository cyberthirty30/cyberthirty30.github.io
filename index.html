About Certifiedboat

Certifiedboat Records, often credited as Certifiedboat, is a Malawian record label and record producer collective. It was founded in 2022 by Malawian artist YBK Sniper, who is the chief executive officer (CEO). The producer collective consists of YBK Sniper himself, YBK Glock, and GeeSlime. In 2022, Malawian artist YBK Sniper started Certifiedboat Records. YBK Sniper believes that Certifiedboat is a way to help producers make money off of music using the same techniques he uses.
